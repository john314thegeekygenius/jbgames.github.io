About JB Games

In 2014, Jonathan Clevenger and his brother Benjamin Clevenger, decided to collaborate on making games together.
Many of the games from JB Games are ment to give players that 90's MS-DOS 256 color VGA experience in a modern era.


Our Vision

We here at JB Games want to make fun games, while still giving players that 90's feel by using a limited amount of colors.
Games that follow this pattern are supported by the Meeks Sandbox Engine. This is NOT an emulator. It is a type of API that allows our games to run on almost any platform.
